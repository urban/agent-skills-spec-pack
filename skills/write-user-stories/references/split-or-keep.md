# Split or keep?

Split a story when:

- it has multiple actions
- it has multiple outcomes
- it describes a full feature epic rather than one buildable slice
- different actors would care about different parts of it
- one part could be implemented or tested without the other
- one part is happy-path and another is boundary or failure behavior

Keep a story together when:

- it describes one meaningful behavior for one actor in one situation
- the action and outcome stay tightly coupled
- the observation is singular and clear
